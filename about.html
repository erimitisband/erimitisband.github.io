The Story

ERIMITIS

Erimitis is a Melbourne music project built for those who still believe music should mean something.

Drawing from every decade since the 1960s: the atmosphere of Floyd, the riff and thunder of Zeppelin, the melody and soul of The Beatles, the storytelling of Dire Straits, the iron will of Maiden, the darkness of Sabbath, the electric soul of Hendrix, the blues of Clapton, the swagger of The Rolling Stones and the voltage of AC/DC — Erimitis is not a tribute to the past. It is something that belongs to now.

"Raw power, emotional depth, and guitar-driven storytelling woven into something that belongs to now."
