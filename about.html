ABOUT

We exist in the friction between the heavy and the ethereal. A collective shadow reborn in the silence of the iron woods, weaving together textures of light and immense weight.

Our sound is a dialogue between eras, drawing deep inspiration from the melodic mastery of The Beatles, the atmospheric depths of Wardruna, and the raw, rhythmic intensity of Mgła.
