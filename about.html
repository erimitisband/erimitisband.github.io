ERIMITIS

Erimitis is a haunting architecture of sound—a singular enigma woven from the echoes of the giants who paved the way. We inhabit the space where melody meets shadow.

Our DNA is forged in the melodic alchemy of The Beatles and the primal, heavy thunder of Sabbath and Zeppelin. It is painted with the psychedelic lunar vastness of Floyd and the raw, electric spirit of Hendrix.

Defined by the iron-clad gallop of Maiden and the high-voltage grit of AC/DC, we find our soul in the precise, timeless elegance of Clapton and Dire Straits.
