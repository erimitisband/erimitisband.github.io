The Story

Erimitis is a Melbourne music project built for those who still believe music should mean something.

Drawing from every decade since the 1960s — the atmosphere of Floyd, the riff and thunder of Zeppelin, the melody and soul of The Beatles, the storytelling of Dire Straits, the iron will of Maiden, the darkness of Sabbath - Erimitis is not a tribute to the past, it is something that belongs to now.

The foundation is in place. The vision is clear. The right people are being found.
